I scream for ICE CREAM

Description

This is the ice_cream webpage I created for the odin-recipes assignment of the Odin Project.

Description: White Coffee ice cream offers a luscious blend of creamy, delicate white chocolate and the robust, smooth taste of coffee, resulting in a harmonious and indulgent flavor that delights with every spoonful.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons instant coffee granules
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup white chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Prepare Ice Cream Base
  3. Add Coffee Flavor
  4. Incorporate Vanilla
  5. Churn the Ice Cream
  6. Add White Chocolate
